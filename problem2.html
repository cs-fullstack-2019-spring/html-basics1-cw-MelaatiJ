About Melaati J

Melaati .J was born in Sri-Lanka September 21st 1995. She traveled all the way to America with her older brother and her two parents at the age of 4 years old. Their first stop was Boston, Massachusetts. She lived there for a year and traveled to Memphis, Tennessee. She is currently 23 years old and has embarked on her journey to becomee a software developer.

Melaati J was a restaurant manager for 5 years at a restaurant called Red Koi Japanese Cuisine. She decided that was not the route she wished to take any longer.
